# Focus Management 
## Question 1 - What is Deep Work?
- Deep Work means giving your full attention to one important task.
- You work in a place with no distractions where there will be no phone, no notifications, no multitasking.
- Your mind becomes fully engaged, so you can think clearly and creatively.
- It helps us produce better results in less time.
- It also helps us learn faster and improve our skills more deeply.

## Question 2 - According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
- Pick one meaningful task you want to give your full attention to.
- Give yourself at least an hour to focus without interruptions.
- Start the session knowing exactly what you want to get done.
- Work in a clean, quiet place where your mind feels calm.
- Take a break afterward to relax and recharge your mind.

## Question 3 - How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
- Set a small focus time every day where you work without distractions, even if it’s just 30–60 minutes.
- Begin your day by choosing the one task that matters the most and do that first.
- Give yourself a short break after your deep work session to relax and recharge.
- Slowly increase your deep work time as your ability to concentrate grows.
- Close all extra tabs and apps to keep your mind clear and calm.

## Question 4 - What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
- It reduces our ability to focus and stay present.
- It is designed to be addictive and consumes our time.
- It increases stress, comparison, and feelings of inadequacy.
- It can harm mental health and lead to anxiety or loneliness.
- It distracts you from meaningful, deep work and personal goals.
